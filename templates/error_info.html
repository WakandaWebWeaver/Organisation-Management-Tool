Welcome to Self-Help Section

Here, you will find all the questions, errors, and what to do when you get those errors.

  1. Error 404:
    • Page not found.
    • Check the URL and try again.
  2. Cannot access your downloads or files?
    • There might be a problem with the server. Please wait and try again.
    • Rest assured, your files are safe, they are merely inaccessible.
  3. Cannot access the website?
    • Are you logged in? If you are, try logging out and logging back in. If the problem persists, contact the website admin.
